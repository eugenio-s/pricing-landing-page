I'm a web designer based in Scotland, and I focus on one thing: building websites that help small businesses get more customers.

I started INEO because I saw too many local businesses getting overcharged by agencies or stuck with templated sites that don't represent them. You deserve better than that.

Every project gets my full attention—you work directly with me from start to finish. No account managers, no back-and-forth with strangers. Just honest conversations about what your business needs.

How It Works

Three simple steps. No surprises.

1

We Talk

Quick chat about your business and what you need. Free, relaxed conversation—no pressure, no jargon. If we're a good fit, I'll send you a simple questionnaire to gather what I need.

2

I Build

You send me your text and photos (I'll help if you're stuck). I create your site and share progress every couple of days. You tell me what works and what doesn't—we adjust together.

3

You Launch

Site goes live. I submit it to Google and walk you through how to make updates. You get 1 week of free support, then I'm always around if you need me.
