INEO is a web design service operated by Eugenio Strods, based in Scotland. This policy explains how I handle your personal information in accordance with UK data protection law (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Information I Collect

When you contact me through this website, I collect:

  • The contents of your message

How I Use Your Information

I use your information solely to:

The legal basis for processing is your consent (when you submit the contact form) and legitimate business interests (to respond to enquiries and deliver services).

I Don't Share Your Data

Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security & Storage

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree, a secure form handling service. I retain enquiry data for up to 2 years, or longer if you become a client (for the duration of our working relationship plus 6 years for legal/accounting purposes).

Cookies

This website uses a single, essential cookie to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode). No tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies are used.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing

  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, email me at eugeniostrods@gmail.com.
