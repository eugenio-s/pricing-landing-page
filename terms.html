Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2026

The Short Version

I build websites. You pay for them. We both treat each other fairly. That's it.

Payment Terms

  • 50% deposit required to begin work
  • 50% balance due upon completion, before site goes live
  • Payment via bank transfer or PayPal
  • Invoices are due within 7 days

What's Included

  • Custom website design and development
  • Mobile-responsive layout
  • 3 rounds of revisions
  • Basic training on how to update your site
  • 1 week of post-launch support

What's Not Included

  • Domain registration (I can help you set this up)
  • Hosting fees (I can recommend providers)
  • Stock photos or custom photography
  • Ongoing maintenance (available as add-on)
  • Content writing (you provide the text)

Revisions

You get 3 revision rounds included in every project. A revision round is a single batch of changes sent together. Additional revisions are charged at £40/hour.

Timeline

Most projects take 5-7 working days. This depends on you providing content and feedback promptly. Delays on your end may extend the timeline.

Ownership

Once paid in full, you own your website. I retain the right to showcase it in my portfolio unless you request otherwise.

Refunds

The 50% deposit is non-refundable once work begins. If you're unhappy with the direction, we'll work together to fix it within your revision rounds.

Cancellation

Either party can cancel with written notice. You'll pay for work completed up to that point.
